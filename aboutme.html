Background

I was born in 1999 in Rauma, Finland. I graduated from upper secondary school in the spring of 2018 with Eximia cum laude approbatur. I started university the next autumn in Aalto with a major in Information networks and moved to Helsinki.

After six months of studying, I left for a year to do my voluntary military service for women in the Conscript Band of Finland. Now I’m returned to school with a new kind of motivation toward studying.

Happy to be back!

Character

I would say my best qualities are that I’m decisive, care-free and a fast learner. My peers and superiors have described me as a good coworker, since I’m always ready to help and I get things done. I’m also really interested in learning new skills.

I’m trying to improve myself in stress and time management. I don’t stress often, but when I do, I feel like there’s a big knot in my brain. I’m trying to learn how to undo that knot.
