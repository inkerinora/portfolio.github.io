Arranging projects

I had probably arranged once before my conscription, but after last year, I can say I have some experience in arranging. I mostly did five-horn arrangements for Showband, but when our conscription came to and end, I got to arrange the "symphonic" winds for the chorus of our "tj0"-song. It was a song we did in honor of our last day in the Conscript Band of Finland. I also play the baritone saxophone on it.
