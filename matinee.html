Syksyn värejä -matinees

Here's some of the pictures I took during one of the matinees!

Syksyn värejä (The Colours Of Autumn) was the first project that I was in charge of, regarding the marketing, communications and visual side of the project. I did the poster (which was the first poster I have ever made), the program, wrote the briefing and sent to local news... I also photographed the matinees and handed the programs to the audience. I think I did pretty good considering that I had no former experience on this kind of work.
